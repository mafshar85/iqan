
👉 To start the proxy, run:
   hysteria client -c ~/hysteria/client.yaml

🛰️ To view your connection share link, run:
   hysteria share -c ~/hysteria/client.yaml

📌 Tip: Keep your config secure and test the proxy with:
   curl --socks5 127.0.0.1:1080 https://example.com

📌 Tip: To set HTTP proxy in your terminal session:
   export http_proxy=http://127.0.0.1:1099 #hysteria_port
   export https_proxy=http://127.0.0.1:1099 #hysteria_port
